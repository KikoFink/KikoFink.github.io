Explore Monte Barro

Monte Barro is a mountain located in Lombardy, Italy, with an elevation of 922 meters.2 It rises from the plain between Lecco, Valmadrera, and Civate, and is bordered by the Lakes of Garlate and Annone. The southern side of the mountain ends on the saddle of Galbiate, where the ridge of Monte Brianza starts.1 Monte Barro is a beautiful and charming terrace overlooking the Brianza area and its lakes, located in front of the city of Lecco, southwest of Grignes. It is surrounded by Lake Como, Lake Annone, and Lake Garlate, and is placed above Galbiate. It seems a protector of the town of Lecco, covering the marvellous panorama to the North. The property is surrounded by hills and lakes, within 5 minutes' ride away from Monte Barro Mountain. The accommodation is also nestled within spitting distance of shops and a supermarket, and St Nicholas Campanile lies merely 1.6 km away.
